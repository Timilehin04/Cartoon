Hi! I'm Kenny!

I'm the Kenny behind Kehinde Studio! I'm a furry creator with a hearing impairment and a passion for character-centric art! Welcome to my corner of the world!

I've always been an artsy person. It brings me great joy to share my talents with others and bring happiness and excitement into their world. Using my knowledge in animation, illustration, and even tailoring, I sought to bring other's visions of characters to life with a uniquely animated spark!

After earning my Animation degree in 2021, I turned this business into a full-time venture! To this day I continue to strive for the most unique, most resilient, most adorable results possible, in all forms of art I pursue, but especially in that of the fursuit variety! It's been quite the journey since our humble beginnings in 2019, and I am eagerly looking forward to keep giving back to the amazing furry fandom!
